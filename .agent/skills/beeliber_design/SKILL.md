# 🎨 Beeliber Design & Brand Excellence

This skill encapsulates the visual and emotional identity of Beeliber, ensuring every UI element feels premium and trustworthy.

## 💎 1. Visual Identity Standards

- **Color Palette**:
  - Primary: `#FFC700` (Beeliber Yellow)
  - Accent: `#000000` (Trust Black)
  - Background: `#FFFFFF` (Pure White)
  - Secondary: `#F5F5F0` (Warm Gray) for cards
- **Typography**:
  - Font: 'Pretendard' or 'Inter' (Bold).
  - Styling: Strictly use `letter-spacing: -0.05em` for headlines to project "solid trust."
- **Logo Detail**: The "liber" part of the logo must be italicized at exactly 15 degrees.

## 🚀 2. UX Renewal Strategy

- **Mood**: "Warm & Bright." Avoid dark/gloomy filters.
- **Terminology (Crucial)**:
  - **DON'T** use 'Storage' (보관) if possible.
  - **DO** use 'Freedom' (자유) or 'Delivery' (배달/배송).
- **Hero Section**: Focus on "hands-free" liberation. Use natural sunlight imagery.
- **Context-Aware UI**: Priority actions (Drop-off in morning, Pick-up in evening) should be highlighted based on time and GPS.

## ✅ 3. Quality Checklist (Do & Don't)

- **Do**: Use 15-degree italics for brand accents. Use 🐝 emoji in CTA and support buttons.
- **Don't**: Show heavy luggage or tired people. Keep it light and aspirational.
- **Multi-language**: Apply cultural filters (e.g., emphasizing "Safety" for Japanese users).

> "It's not just a service; it's a gift of freedom. Design it that way. 💅✨"
